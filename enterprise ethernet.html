High CoS

Type: Committed 1:1

All data allocated to this class of service is given priority across the nbn™ network over best efforts residential and business traffic. This is the highest quality data on Enterprise Ethernet with commitments to customers on network latency, jitter and frame delay.

Low CoS

Type:Best Efforts

Speeds include best effort contention and are not guaranteed. This is best for Web Browsing, File Transfer, E-Commerce and E-mail. Speeds can be affected by factors such as, network congestion, on-premise equipment, the remote party being accessed, and fibre transmission overheads.
