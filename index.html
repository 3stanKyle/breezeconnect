General Information for All Plans

  • Service Description:
    • Delivered on the nbn™ network
    • Technologies: FTTP, FTTB, FTTN, or HFC (Premium plans use FTTP only)
  • Speed Information:
    • Listed speeds are maximum possible during off-peak periods
    • Actual speeds may vary
  • Contract Terms:
    • Minimum term: 1 month
    • Cancellation: Email help@breezeconnect.com.au
  • Additional Features:
    • Static WAN IP address available for $4.95/month (required for onsite server hosting)
  • Equipment Requirements:
    • NBN-ready modem/router required
    • FTTC customers: Network Connection Device needed
    • HFC customers: NBN Termination Device needed (provided free by NBNCo)
  • Full Terms: Available at breezeconnect.com.au/customer-terms
